The site was integrated with paypal, enabling visitors to the site to buy driving lessons over the internet with their paypal account.

We also decided to go for a redesign as well to make it brighter and more inviting to users.  The original html and content was kept exactly the same and extra content such as the recently passed carousel was added to the top of the page.
